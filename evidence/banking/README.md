# Banking Evidence Pack (v1)

## Risk distribution summary
- low: 6
- medium: 4
- high: 2

## Representative examples

### Example 1 (risky -> softened)
Input:
- "This product will give you excellent returns and is risk-free. You should buy."

Output:
- "This product may give you excellent returns and is lower-risk. you may wish to buy.

Disclaimer: This message is for information purposes only and does not constitute investment advice. Any decision should be based on your risk profile and suitability assessment."
