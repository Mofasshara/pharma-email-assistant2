What this service does
- Provides a structured review output for banking rewrites.
- Normalizes compliance outcomes separate from generation.

Who uses it
- Risk teams
- Compliance reviewers
- Audit and governance stakeholders

Why it exists separately from rewrite
- Keeps generation and review concerns decoupled.
- Allows policy review to evolve without changing rewrite logic.

Why this matters
- Banks separate generation from review.
- This models real governance architecture.
